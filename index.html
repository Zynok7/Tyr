How to Use

  1. Click the "Copy Script" button on any script card above.
  2. Open your preferred Roblox Executor (e.g., Wave, Synapse, Electron, Solara).
  3. Paste (Ctrl + V) the script into the executor code box.
  4. Inject/Attach your executor into the Roblox client game.
  5. Press Execute and enjoy the GUI dashboard in-game!
